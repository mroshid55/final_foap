## Overview

**FOAP Project** is a web-based platform that manages events, projects, teams, donations, volunteers, newsletters, and more. This project includes features such as contact management, social media integration, galleries, and sign-up forms for users.

It is designed to streamline communication, engagement, and collaboration for organizations and communities.

---

## Features

- **Home Page:** Social media links updated for easy access.
- **About Us:** Keep your audience informed with organizational details.
- **Contact:** Integrated contact apps for user interaction.
- **Donate:** Secure donation management with social link updates.
- **Events:** Manage and display upcoming events.
- **Gallery:** Showcase images with social media integration.
- **Newsletters:** Collect sign-up emails to reach your audience.
- **Program:** Highlight organizational programs.
- **Projects:** Display and manage various projects.
- **Sponsor:** Manage sponsorship information and links.
- **Team:** Display team members and social links.
- **Volunteer:** Volunteer sign-up and management.
- **Templates & Static Files:** Pre-built templates and static resources included.
